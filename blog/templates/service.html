    Tiles Installation Services

Our tiling division has a team of experienced and dedicated tilers who can make that perfect finish you will admire in your house, shopping mall, residential estate amongst others.

We provide a full tiling service which includes but is not limited the following:

  • Floor and wall tiling in ceramic, porcelain, natural stone, mosaic
  • Bullnosing and mitreing of tiles
  • Inlays and patterns
  • Removal of old tiles
  • Surface preparation
  • Waterproofing of shower walls & floors
  • Cleaning, re-grouting and sealing of tiles
      • Pavements Installation Services

    SA Pavers and Tilers offers a comprehensive range of paving solutions for residential, commercial and industrial projects. Our team of designers and installers have extensive experience of delivering professional services to valued clients.

      Our paving services ranges from:
  • Car parks and driveways;
  • Walkways and sidewalks;
  • Patios;
  • Cleaning services and general maintenance

    Whether you plan to sell your property or keep it for generations, a pavement surface installed by SA Pavers and Tilers will provide quality and long lasting results that will quickly become the envy of your neighbours.
